Rollo — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

Overview

Rollo is a local multiplayer game that runs entirely on your device. Your privacy is important to us, and we have designed the app with that in mind.

Data Collection

Rollo does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app does not require an internet connection and does not communicate with any servers.

Analytics and Tracking

Rollo does not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking frameworks.

Local Storage

Rollo stores your game preferences (such as color themes and game mode) locally on your device using standard system settings. This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

Rollo does not integrate with any third-party services.

Children's Privacy

Rollo does not collect any data from any user, including children.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update this page with the revised policy.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us via the support page.
