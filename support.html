How do I play?

Rollo is a local multiplayer game for two players on the same device. Each player takes turns launching pieces from their side of the board, trying to push them into the scoring zone in the center.

How do I change colors or game mode?

Tap the settings icon to choose between Ball Game and Smiley Game modes, and pick your preferred color theme for each player.

The app isn't working correctly

Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, please contact us at the email above with a description of the problem and your device model and iOS version.
